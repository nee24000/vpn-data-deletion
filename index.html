Data Deletion Request - Tongeight VPN

This page explains how users of Tongeight VPN can request deletion of their data.

How to Request Data Deletion

  1. Send an email to: wsinw14@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: "Data Deletion Request"
  3. Include your device information or any relevant details to help us identify your data.

Data We May Process

  • Temporary connection logs (if stored)
  • Device identifiers
  • Diagnostic or crash data

Data Retention Policy

Temporary connection logs are automatically deleted within 90 days. We do not store browsing history or monitor user activity.

Data deletion requests will be processed within 7 business days after verification.
