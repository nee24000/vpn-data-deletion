Data Deletion Request - YOUR APP NAME

If you would like to request deletion of your data associated with YOUR APP NAME, please follow the steps below:

How to request data deletion:

  1. Send an email to: support@yourdomain.com
  2. Use the subject: "Data Deletion Request"
  3. Include your device ID (if applicable)

What data we delete:

  • Connection logs (if stored)
  • Device identifiers
  • Diagnostic data

Data retention:

Temporary connection logs are automatically deleted within 90 days. We do not store browsing history.

Requests will be processed within 7 business days.
